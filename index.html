Eliminate

  • Sugar in all forms, including sucrose, fructose, high fructose corn syrup. Natural low-impact sweeteners such as stevia & polyols (e.g., xylitol & erythritol) are allowed.
  • Artificial sweeteners (sucralose, maltodextrin, saccharin, aspartame, Sweet 'n' Low, Splenda, Equal)
  • Gluten-containing grains: avoid foods which contain wheat, oats, rye, spelt, kamut, bulgur, couscous, and barley. This includes pastas, bread, crackers, cereals, and other products made from these grains. Gluten Free whole grains such as brown rice, wild rice, millet, quinoa, amaranth, and buckwheat are allowable during the detox program
  • Dairy products including milk, cheese, yogurt, and ice cream
  • All alcohol and caffeine-containing beverages including coffee, tea and soda
  • Soy protein, including tofu and tempeh
  • Desserts (chocolate. candy, cakes, cookies)
  • Fried foods, hydrogenated oils, margarine
  • Peanuts/peanut butter (high allergen legumes)
  • Very Important Drink as much water as possible while on this detox. Recommended 1 gallon a day of purified/distilled water.

Detoxification Program Guidelines

21 Day Simple Schedule

Directions:

The directions have been written as simply as possible for your convenience. You will consume two Bodycor Powerplant protein shakes (split them up throughout the day) Bodycor Super Vitamin, and one healthy meal that is the most convenient to your lifestyle. Snacks are also ok, please see notes regarding snacks. We recommend starting the first TWO days of this detox also using Bodycor VITAL CLEANSE to really help you jump start your detox. The VITAL CLEANSE will help you eliminate a lot of old waste, so be advised how and when you take it. After the two days, we leave it up to you to decide if you need to continue using it while on the detox program.

Example of your daily meal intake.

Breakfast

Bodycor Plantpower protein, one single scoop in water, or your choice of unsweetened almond milk, coconut milk, or other milk alternative.

Lunch

Regular Meal: Choose healthy items suggested from the optimal food choices. Bodycor Super Vitamin supplement is recommended at lunch as it’s best taken with food.

Dinner

Bodycor Plantpower or DivaTrim IsoLean protein, single scoop, in water or your choice of unsweetened almond milk, coconut milk, or other milk alternative.

Snacks

This is not a calorie restrictive program. If you feel hungry between meals, you can snack on healthy whole foods in moderation. However, tune in to your body and make sure that you are truly hungry and not just bored, tired, or stressed!
